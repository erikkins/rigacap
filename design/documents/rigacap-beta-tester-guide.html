Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see a clean dashboard with everything you need. Here's what each section does:

Buy Signals

Fresh signals (green BUY badge) are new opportunities the Ensemble strategy identified today. Monitoring signals (gray) are stocks being watched for potential entry. Focus on the fresh signals first.

Open Positions

Shows currently tracked positions with real-time P&L, entry price, trailing stop level, and high water mark. The trailing stop automatically adjusts as positions move in your favor.

Simple / Advanced Mode

Toggle in the top-right corner. Simple mode shows essential signals and positions. Advanced mode adds momentum scores, regime data, and detailed analytics.

Market Regime

The current market regime (e.g., Strong Bull, Range Bound, Weak Bear) is displayed at the top. The strategy adapts its parameters automatically based on which of the 7 regimes is active.

Daily Workflow

RigaCap is designed to fit your schedule. Here's how a typical day works:

4:00 PM ET — Daily Scan

After the market closes, our system scans the full stock universe and runs the Ensemble strategy. New signals and position updates appear on your dashboard.

6:00 PM ET — Email Digest

A beautifully formatted email lands in your inbox with today's top signals, current positions with P&L, and any watchlist updates. Everything you need in one glance.

Intraday — Sell Alerts

If a trailing stop is triggered during market hours, you'll receive an immediate email alert so you can act promptly through your broker.

Your Action

Review the signals, do your own research, and decide whether to trade. Log in to your broker and execute any trades you choose. RigaCap identifies opportunities — you make the decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real money?

The signals are generated from real market data in real time. Whether you choose to trade based on them is entirely your decision. RigaCap does not manage funds, place orders, or hold any of your money.

How many signals should I expect per day?

Typically 2–4 fresh signals on active market days. Some days — especially during weak market regimes — there may be none. Quality over quantity is a core principle.

What broker should I use?

Any US brokerage works — Schwab, Fidelity, Interactive Brokers, TD Ameritrade, Robinhood, etc. RigaCap is broker-agnostic. You execute all trades yourself through your own account.

Will I be charged?

No. Your beta tester account is fully comped. You will not be billed during the beta period.

How long is the beta period?

We'll let you know as we approach public launch. You'll receive advance notice before any changes to your account status.

Can I share my account?

Please don't. Each beta account is tied to one person so we can correlate feedback with the specific experience you're seeing.
