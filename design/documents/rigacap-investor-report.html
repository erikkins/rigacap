How It Works: The Ensemble Approach

RigaCap's production strategy requires three independent factors to align before entering a trade, dramatically reducing false signals:

1. Breakout Timing

Price must clear its long-term accumulation zone — a proprietary breakout signal refined from 127 legacy stored procedures and 20+ years of data.

2. Momentum Quality

Stock must rank in the top momentum tier via a composite score: short (10d) × 0.5 + long (60d) × 0.3 − volatility × 0.2.

3. Volume Confirmation

Requires 1.3× average volume spike and proximity to 50-day high — institutional participation confirmed.

4. Adaptive Exit

12% trailing stop from high water mark, adjusted by market regime (8–20%). Winners run; losers are cut quickly.

Portfolio Construction: Maximum 6 concentrated positions at 15% allocation each. Biweekly rebalancing. This concentration in high-conviction signals is a key driver of outperformance vs. diversified approaches.

AI & Machine Learning

Parameter Discovery & Validation

RigaCap's parameters were discovered through a tournament-based approach: each parameter was tested independently across its full range, isolating which levers actually move returns. This identified that entry quality (breakout proximity) is the dominant factor — not stop levels, position sizing, or regime-adaptive adjustments.

Walk-forward testing across 50+ configurations validated that fixed parameters outperform AI-optimized parameters by a wide margin. Bayesian optimizers (Optuna TPE) were tested extensively but consistently added noise without improving returns. The final strategy uses a single, robust parameter set across all market conditions.

Binary Regime Response

Rather than adjusting parameters per regime (which was tested and reduced returns by -18pp annualized), the system uses a binary approach: invested (SPY > 200-day MA) or cash (SPY < 200-day MA). This simple rule kept the portfolio positive during the entire 2022 bear market (flat while SPY fell -20%).

Walk-Forward Validation (Anti-Overfitting)

The system was validated across 10 years of history (2016–2026) using walk-forward methodology. At each biweekly boundary, the system trades forward into unseen data using fixed parameters. This produces an out-of-sample equity curve — the +603% return is entirely on data the system never saw during development. The strategy was further stress-tested across 7 different start dates — every one positive, every one beats the S&P 500.

Metrics tracked per period: Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar, profit factor, win rate, max consecutive losses, recovery factor, Ulcer index. The system logs every parameter change and strategy switch for full investor transparency.
