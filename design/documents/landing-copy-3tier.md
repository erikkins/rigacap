## WHY THE DIAL (differentiators, 3 cards)

1. **Adaptive, not static.** We read the market's regime and *change what we do* — offense in
   orderly trends, defense in turbulence. One-size-fits-all strategies can't.
2. **A crash brake that watches the right thing.** The 2021 momentum crash was invisible to
   VIX and the index. We defend against it by watching *the momentum factor's own
   volatility* — a warning light nobody else is reading.
3. **Honesty is the product.** We tell you each tier's cost up front. The Preserver trails in
   a melt-up; the Maximizer owns a crash tail. We'd rather you pick right than sell you
   the wrong thing.

## FAQ

**Q: How is this different from every other signal service?**
Most run one strategy in all weather. We run a *regime-adaptive* engine and let you set the
risk — from capital preservation to aggressive growth — on the same disciplined core.

**Q: Do you manage my money?**
No. We send signals; you execute at your own broker. You're always in control of your money.

**Q: Can I switch tiers?**
Anytime. Switching rotates your holdings toward the new tier, so we'll confirm before you do
— we don't want you tier-chasing (that's the bad behavior we exist to prevent).

**Q: Can I build a custom blend?**
The three tiers span the risk/reward range and fit almost everyone. A fully custom blend is
on the roadmap for advisers managing client sleeves.

**Q: What's the catch on the aggressive tier?**
Momentum strategies have one classic failure mode — a "momentum crash" (2021 is the textbook
case). Maximizer carries that risk. We don't hide it; we built a mechanical volatility
brake that roughly halves it. You should only pick this tier if a rough year for the trade
is one you can stomach.

**Q: Is this financial advice?**
No — signals and education only. You make your own decisions. *(+ standard disclaimer.)*

**Q: Who is this NOT for?**
If you want to beat the market every single quarter, no strategy here (or anywhere honestly)
does that. We optimize risk-adjusted returns and drawdown control over full cycles.
